About me

I’m Ollie Danvers owner and coach at H.I.P.P.O Triathlon Coaching. A family man with 2 kids so I understand the fine balance of work/life/training. I’ve been teaching, coaching and mentoring new and trained soldiers within the British Army for the past 10 years. whether it be basic training or physical development.

I’ve taken all my knowledge and experience and brought it to triathlon coaching. I have a love for the sport and it shows in my coaching style. A desire to work alongside athletes to help them achieve goals. I will not claim to know everything but what I do know is that I can help anyone achieve their goals.

I’ve been coached and mentored by some fantastic coaches who have shown me different ways and given me different tools to to enhance my coaching knowledge.
